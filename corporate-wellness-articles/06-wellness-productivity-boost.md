## Introduction

The World Health Organization reports that employees with high well-being are 21% more productive than those with low well-being.

This isn't correlation—it's causation. Better physical health, mental wellness, and recovery directly translate to cognitive function, energy, focus, and sustained performance.

**This article explores the evidence connecting wellness to productivity.**

## Sleep and Productivity

### The Sleep-Performance Connection

| Sleep Quality | Productivity Impact |
|--------------|---------------------|
| Excellent (7-9 hours quality sleep) | Optimal |
| Good (6-7 hours quality) | 5-10% reduction |
| Poor (< 6 hours or disrupted) | 15-25% reduction |
| Very poor (< 5 hours) | 30%+ reduction |

### Cognitive Effects of Poor Sleep

| Cognitive Function | Impact of Poor Sleep |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Attention | 30-40% decline |
| Working memory | 20-30% decline |
| Decision making | 25-35% decline |
| Reaction time | 20-25% slower |
| Creative thinking | 30-50% decline |

### Wellness Program Impact

**Sleep improvement programs can:**
- Improve sleep quality 20-30%
- Reduce fatigue 25%
- Boost afternoon productivity 15-20%
- Reduce errors 20-30%

## Stress and Cognitive Performance

| Stress Level | Cognitive Impact |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Optimal | Peak performance |
| Moderate | Some focus improvement |

### Stress-Productivity Mechanisms

| Mechanism | Effect |

| Rumination | Distraction from tasks |

| Emotional reactivity | Interpersonal friction |

- 15-20% productivity improvement

- Improved collaboration

## Mental Health and Performance

### Depression's Productivity Impact

**Major depression reduces productivity by:**
- 20-30% during mild episodes
- 40-50% during moderate episodes
- 60-80% during severe episodes

### Anxiety's Impact

**Anxiety disorders reduce productivity through:**
- Worry-based distraction
- Avoidance behaviors
- Sleep disruption
- Physical symptoms

### Mental Health Support ROI

| Investment | Return |
|-----------|--------|
| EAP usage | 20-30% productivity recovery |

| Anxiety treatment | 40-60% productivity recovery |
