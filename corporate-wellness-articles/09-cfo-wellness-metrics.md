## Introduction

Wellness programs represent significant investment. CFOs need clear financial metrics, not feel-good stories about employee happiness.

This guide presents wellness in financial terms: investment, return, timeline, and measurement methodology suitable for board presentations.

## The Financial Framework

### Investment Categories

| Category | Typical Range | Notes |
|----------|---------------|-------|
| Platform/technology | $30-100/employee/year | Core analytics |
| Devices | $100-300/employee (Y1) | Wearables, optional |
| Incentives | $100-500/employee/year | Engagement drivers |
| Coaching/programs | $50-200/employee/year | Intervention delivery |
| Administration | $20-50/employee/year | Internal resources |

### Return Categories

| Category | Measurement |
|----------|-------------|
| Healthcare costs | Medical claims reduction |
| Absenteeism | Sick day reduction |
| Productivity | Presenteeism improvement |
| Turnover | Retention improvement |
| Risk mitigation | Avoided costs |

## Healthcare Cost Metrics

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Definition | Target Improvement |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| PEPM | Per-employee per-month claims | 5-15% reduction |
| Trend | YoY claims change | Below industry |
| High-cost claimants | Members >$50K | Reduce by 10-20% |
| ER utilization | ER visits per 1000 | Reduce by 15-25% |
| Hospital admissions | Admits per 1000 | Reduce by 10-20% |

### Calculation Example

**1,000-employee company:**
- Current PEPM: $500
- Annual healthcare spend: $6M
- 10% reduction: $600K savings
- Program cost: $250K
- **Net savings: $350K (2.4:1 ROI)**

## Productivity Metrics

### Measuring Lost Productivity

| Factor | Measurement Method | Cost Calculation |
|--------|-------------------|------------------|
| Absenteeism | Sick days × daily rate | Direct salary cost |
| Presenteeism | Survey × salary % | Productivity loss |
| Disability | STD/LTD days × cost | Benefit + replacement |

### Typical Improvements

| Metric | Expected Improvement | Value |
|--------|---------------------|-------|
| Sick days | 2-3 fewer/employee | $400-750 |
| Presenteeism | 5-10% improvement | $1,000-2,500 |
| Disability | 10-15% reduction | Variable |

## Risk Assessment

### Risk of Investing

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Low participation | Incentive design, communication |
| Slow results | 3-year commitment, realistic expectations |
| Platform failure | Vendor selection, contracts |
| Privacy backlash | Strong policies, transparency |

### Risk of NOT Investing

| Risk | Quantified Impact |
|------|------------------|
| Healthcare cost trend | +5-8%/year |
| Competitive disadvantage | Talent loss |
| Productivity loss | Estimated $X/year |
| Turnover cost | Estimated $X/year |

## Key Takeaways

1. **Quantify everything:** Healthcare, productivity, turnover
2. **Use conservative projections:** Build credibility
3. **Show 3-year view:** Full returns take time
4. **Include sensitivity analysis:** Range of outcomes
5. **Address risks:** Both investing and not investing
6. **Establish metrics early:** Track from day one
7. **Report regularly:** Quarterly dashboards
8. **Compare to projections:** Adjust and improve
