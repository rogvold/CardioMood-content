## Introduction

When employees use a wellness app, whose brand do they see? A white-label platform lets you present wellness as your company's initiative, building employer brand equity while leveraging proven technology.

---

## What Is White-Label Wellness

### Definition

**White-label:** Technology platform rebranded with your company identity, appearing as your own solution rather than a third-party vendor.

### Standard vs. White-Label

| Element | Standard | White-Label |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| App name | Vendor brand | Your brand |
| Colors/design | Vendor standard | Your identity |
| Communications | Vendor branded | Your messaging |
| Integration | Standalone | Part of your ecosystem |
