## Introduction

Wellness programs that work in New York may not work in Tokyo. Global deployment requires navigating cultural differences, regulatory requirements, and operational complexity while maintaining program coherence.

---

## Global Considerations

### Cultural Adaptation

| Region | Wellness Considerations |
|--------|------------------------|
| North America | Individual achievement, competition |
| Europe | Work-life balance, privacy |
| Asia | Group harmony, saving face |
| Latin America | Social connection, family |

### Regulatory Requirements

| Region | Key Considerations |
|--------|-------------------|
| EU/UK | GDPR, strict data protection |
| Americas | HIPAA (US), varied by country |
| APAC | Varied—Singapore strict, others less |
| Middle East | Cultural/religious considerations |

### Language and Content

| Requirement | Approach |

## Operational Considerations

### Time Zones

| Challenge | Solution |
|-----------|----------|
| Live events | Recorded or multiple sessions |
| Challenges | Async participation |
| Support | Regional coverage |
| Analytics | Global dashboard, local detail |

### Data Residency

| Requirement | Compliance |
|-------------|-----------|
| EU data in EU | GDPR requirement |
| Country-specific | Some markets |
| Cross-border | Transfer mechanisms |

---

## Implementation Strategy

### Phased Rollout

| Phase | Scope |
|-------|-------|
| Pilot | Headquarters or main region |
| Wave 1 | Major markets |
| Wave 2 | Secondary markets |
| Wave 3 | All locations |

### Local Champions

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Regional lead | Program oversight |
| Local champion | Culture bridge |
| HR partner | Policy alignment |

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Culture matters:** Adapt to local norms
2. **Compliance required:** Know regional regulations
3. **Unified platform:** Consistency where possible
4. **Local execution:** Flexibility where needed
5. **Time zone aware:** Accessible programming
6. **Data residency:** Meet requirements
7. **Phased rollout:** Learn and adapt
8. **Local champions:** Critical success factor
