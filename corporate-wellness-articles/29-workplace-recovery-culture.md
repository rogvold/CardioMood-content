## Introduction

Some industries are inherently high-stress: healthcare, finance, technology, consulting, emergency services. You can't eliminate the demands—but you can change how people recover from them. A recovery culture sustains performance where burnout culture destroys it.

---

## The Recovery Culture Concept

### Definition

**Recovery culture:** An organizational environment where restoration is valued, enabled, and expected—not just tolerated.

### Culture Comparison

| Burnout Culture | Recovery Culture |
|-----------------|------------------|
| Celebrate overwork | Celebrate smart work |
| Always available | Protected boundaries |
| Rest is weakness | Rest is essential |
| Individual responsibility | Organizational support |
| Push through fatigue | Recognize limits |

### Leadership Behavior

| Action | Message Sent |
|--------|--------------|
