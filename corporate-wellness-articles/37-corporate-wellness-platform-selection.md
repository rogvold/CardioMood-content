## Introduction

The wellness technology market is crowded. Point solutions, comprehensive platforms, wearable-focused, app-only—the options are overwhelming. A structured evaluation process ensures you select a platform that fits your organization's needs, integrates with your systems, and delivers measurable outcomes.

## Evaluation Framework

### Core Criteria

| Category | Weight |
|----------|--------|
| Functionality | 25% |
| Integration | 20% |
| User experience | 20% |
| Security/compliance | 15% |
| Vendor viability | 10% |
| Cost | 10% |

### Needs Assessment

| Question | Purpose |
|----------|---------|

## Key Takeaways

1. **Structured evaluation:** Weight criteria appropriately
2. **Needs first:** Define requirements before evaluating
3. **Integration critical:** Must work with your systems
4. **UX matters:** Adoption depends on usability
5. **Security non-negotiable:** Certifications required
6. **Vendor viability:** Long-term partnership
7. **Total cost:** Beyond subscription price
8. **Reference checks:** Verify claims with customers
