## Introduction

Corporate wellness is evolving rapidly—from annual surveys to continuous biometrics, from one-size-fits-all to personalized interventions, from reactive to predictive. These trends are shaping what employee wellness will look like in 2025 and beyond.

| Quarterly surveys | Real-time feedback |
