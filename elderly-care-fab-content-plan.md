# CardioMood for Elderly Care: FAB Analysis & Content Plan

## FAB Analysis (Features, Advantages, Benefits)

---

### 1. Continuous Health Monitoring

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| 24/7 vital sign tracking via wearable | Captures subtle changes missed by periodic checkups | Early intervention before crises |
| Heart rate and HRV monitoring | Cardiovascular health visibility | Detect cardiac issues days before symptoms |
| Respiratory pattern tracking | Breathing abnormality detection | Early warning of respiratory conditions |
| Comfortable, long-battery wearable | 5-day battery, easy to wear | High compliance among elderly users |

---

### 2. Early Warning Alerts

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| AI detects declining health patterns | 2-3 days warning before visible symptoms | Doctor consultation instead of ER visit |
| Smart alert thresholds | Reduces false alarms | Actionable alerts only |
| Trend-based detection | Pattern recognition over time | Catch gradual decline, not just acute events |
| Escalation protocols | Appropriate response levels | Right care at right time |

---

### 3. Family Portal

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Daily health summaries | 30-second check-in | Peace of mind for adult children |
| Alert-only notifications | No constant monitoring burden | Reduces caregiver anxiety |
| Historical trend views | See patterns over weeks/months | Informed conversations with doctors |
| Mobile-friendly access | Check from anywhere | Caregiver flexibility |

---

### 4. Sleep Quality Tracking

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Sleep stage monitoring | Quality not just duration | 25% sleep quality improvement |
| Breathing pattern analysis | Sleep apnea detection | Prevent falls and cognitive decline |
| Nighttime activity tracking | Wandering/restlessness detection | Safety for dementia patients |
| Sleep trend analysis | Identify declining patterns | Early cognitive decline indicator |

---

### 5. Activity Monitoring

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Daily movement tracking | Mobility trend visibility | 40% reduction in fall injuries |
| Gradual slowdown detection | Weeks of advance warning | Prevent falls before they happen |
| Sedentary pattern alerts | Identify concerning inactivity | Prompt wellness interventions |
| Functional capacity scoring | Objective independence measure | Inform care decisions |

---

### 6. Care Team Coordination

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Data sharing with physicians | Weeks of objective data for visits | More informed medical decisions |
| EHR integration | Seamless clinical workflow | Accepted by healthcare providers |
| Care manager dashboards | Multi-patient visibility | Efficient care facility operations |
| HIPAA compliance | Healthcare-grade privacy | Trusted by medical professionals |
