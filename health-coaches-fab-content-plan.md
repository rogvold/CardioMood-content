# CardioMood for Health Coaches: FAB Analysis & Content Plan

## FAB Analysis (Features, Advantages, Benefits)

---

### 1. Medical-Grade Wearable Device

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| 99.2% PPG accuracy with CE certification | More reliable data than consumer wearables (WHOOP, Oura) | Make confident decisions based on trustworthy data; avoid misguiding clients |
| 19+ clinically validated parameters | Comprehensive health picture vs. 5-7 metrics from competitors | Identify root causes of client issues (fatigue = stress vs. sleep vs. overtraining) |
| 24/7 continuous monitoring | Captures data around the clock, not just during workouts | Spot patterns others miss; understand full lifestyle impact on health |
| Flexible data intervals (25Hz-128Hz) | Research-grade granularity when needed | Conduct deep analysis for complex cases; differentiate from basic coaches |

---

### 2. AI-Powered Analytics Platform

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| AI explains physiological metrics | No medical training required | Start using immediately; expand service offerings without additional education |
| AI suggests evidence-based interventions | Recommendations backed by research | Deliver proven protocols; build credibility with clients |
| AI meal plan generator | Automated nutrition planning | Save hours per client; offer nutrition guidance without being a dietitian |
| Real-time health alerts with priority levels | Proactive issue detection | Catch problems early; demonstrate attentive care; prevent client setbacks |

---

### 3. Coach Dashboard

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Multi-client management | All clients in one view with filtering | Scale practice without losing oversight; manage 50+ clients efficiently |
| Comprehensive visual reporting | Data presented clearly, not raw numbers | Communicate progress effectively to clients; justify coaching value |
| Habit tracking with streaks | Gamified accountability | Higher client compliance; clients see tangible progress |
| Secure HIPAA-compliant messaging | Professional, encrypted communication | Protect client privacy; meet regulatory requirements; build trust |
| Custom questionnaires | Tailored assessments for each client | Personalize approach; gather subjective data alongside objective metrics |
| Scheduling with reminders | Automated appointment management | Reduce no-shows; save admin time; stay organized |

### 4. Client Experience

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
