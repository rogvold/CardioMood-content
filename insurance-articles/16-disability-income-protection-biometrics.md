## Introduction

Disability income protection pays when people can't work. The goal should be keeping people working.

Traditional disability insurance is purely reactive—waiting for claims, then managing them. But the conditions that lead to disability often develop over months or years before someone stops working: burnout, chronic stress, developing musculoskeletal issues, declining mental health.

**Biometric monitoring can detect these trajectories early enough for intervention.**

## Biometric Signals for Disability Risk

### Stress and Burnout Indicators

**Burnout develops over weeks/months with detectable patterns:**

| Metric | Burnout Trajectory |
|--------|-------------------|
| HRV | Declining trend over 4-8 weeks |
| Resting HR | Elevated, rising |
| Sleep quality | Deteriorating |
| Activity | Initially high, then declining |
| Recovery | Incomplete, even on weekends |

**Research finding:** Biometric burnout patterns emerge 3-4 weeks before self-reported symptoms.

### Mental Health Warning Signs

**Anxiety and depression correlate with:**

| Metric | Pattern |
|--------|---------|
| Sleep onset | Delayed (anxiety) |
| Early waking | Common (depression) |
| Activity decline | Gradual reduction |
| Daily variability | Loss of normal patterns |
| Social activity | Declining (where tracked) |

### Musculoskeletal Risk

**Activity patterns indicating MSK risk:**

| Metric | Risk Pattern |
|--------|-------------|
| Sedentary time | > 10 hours/day |
| Activity consistency | Low variability |
| Weekend activity | Not compensating |
| Sleep quality | Poor (pain indicator) |

## Early Intervention Programs

### The Intervention Window

**From early detection to claim:**

| Stage | Timeline | Intervention Opportunity |
|-------|----------|-------------------------|
| Early warning signs | 3-6 months pre-claim | High |
| Symptomatic but working | 1-3 months pre-claim | Moderate |
| Performance impacted | 1-4 weeks pre-claim | Low |
| Unable to work | Claim filed | Reactive only |

**Goal:** Intervene during high-opportunity window.

### Intervention Types

| Intervention | Application |
|--------------|-------------|
| Wellness coaching | Lifestyle modification |
| Stress management | Burnout prevention |
| EAP referral | Mental health support |
| Ergonomic support | MSK prevention |
| Medical referral | Early treatment |
| Workload review | Employer engagement |

### Program Structure

**Effective early intervention program:**

1. **Monitoring:** Continuous biometric tracking
2. **Detection:** AI-based pattern recognition
3. **Assessment:** Human review of flagged cases
4. **Outreach:** Proactive member contact
5. **Intervention:** Appropriate support resource
6. **Follow-up:** Monitor improvement
7. **Escalation:** If intervention insufficient
