## Introduction

Travel insurance protects against trip disruptions, medical emergencies abroad, and adventure mishaps. Traditional travel insurance is priced on destination, duration, and basic demographics.

Wearable biometrics add new dimensions: pre-trip health status, in-trip monitoring, and real-time risk management. This enables personalized pricing, proactive assistance, and smarter claims handling.

**This guide explores biometric applications across the travel insurance lifecycle.**

---

## Pre-Trip Applications

### Health Risk Assessment

**Traditional approach:**
- Self-declared medical conditions
- Age-based loading
- Generic destination risk

**Biometric-enhanced approach:**
- Objective current health status
- Fitness level for planned activities
- Stress and recovery capacity
- Recent health trajectory

### Fitness for Purpose

| Trip Type | Relevant Biometrics |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Adventure/active | Cardiovascular fitness, activity levels |
| Altitude destinations | HRV, resting HR, recovery |
| Extended travel | Sleep patterns, stress capacity |
| Elderly traveler | Functional capacity, fall risk |

### Pre-Trip Risk Scoring

| Risk Factor | Biometric Indicator |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Cardiac event | Low HRV, elevated RHR |
| Heat intolerance | Poor recovery metrics |
| Altitude sickness | Cardiovascular indicators |
| Fatigue-related issues | Sleep quality, stress |

## Pricing Applications

### Dynamic Risk Pricing

**Traditional:**
- Fixed rates by destination
- Age bands
- Pre-existing condition declarations

**Biometric-informed:**
- Individual health assessment
- Activity-appropriate risk
- Trend-adjusted pricing

### Pricing Scenarios

| Scenario | Pricing Adjustment |
|----------|-------------------|
| Fit traveler, adventure trip | Standard to discount |
| Declining metrics, any trip | Consider loading |
| Excellent health, extreme trip | Reduced from base |
| Poor fitness, active trip | Loading or exclusion |

### Incentive Pricing

| Offer | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| Healthy traveler discount | Demonstrated good biometrics |
| Pre-trip wellness bonus | Improved metrics before departure |
| Activity tracking discount | Agree to in-trip monitoring |

## In-Trip Monitoring

**Continuous monitoring enables:**
- Early warning of health issues
- Activity verification for claims

### Use Cases

| Scenario | Biometric Application |
|----------|----------------------|

### Emergency Response

**Biometric-enhanced assistance:**

| Detection | Response |
|-----------|----------|

| Location + vitals | Inform emergency responders |
