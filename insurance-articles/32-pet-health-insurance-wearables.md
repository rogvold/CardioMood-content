## Introduction

Pet insurance is the fastest-growing segment of the insurance market. Pet wearables—once novelty items—are becoming sophisticated health monitors capable of tracking activity, sleep, and vital signs for dogs and cats.

The convergence creates opportunity: wearable data can improve pet health insurance underwriting, claims management, and wellness programs.

**This guide explores biometric applications for pet health insurance.**
