# CardioMood for Insurance: FAB Analysis & Content Plan

## FAB Analysis (Features, Advantages, Benefits)

---

### 1. Continuous Risk Assessment

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| 24/7 biometric monitoring via wearables | Captures health trajectories vs. point-in-time screenings | Detect emerging risks 6-12 months before events |
| ML risk scoring trained on 500M+ data points | Predictive models with massive training dataset | 40% better claims prediction accuracy |
| Real-time health data streaming | Immediate visibility into policyholder health changes | Faster intervention, reduced claim severity |
| HRV, sleep, stress, activity tracking | Multi-dimensional health picture | Identify cardiovascular, stress, and lifestyle risks early |

---

### 2. Preventive Health Programs

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Early at-risk policyholder identification | Intervene before conditions worsen | 18% claims reduction; 50x lower intervention vs. treatment cost |
| Gamification and engagement tools | 3x higher wellness program participation | Build member loyalty; reduce churn |
| Personalized health insights | Tailored recommendations per policyholder | Higher compliance with wellness activities |
| Chronic disease management alerts | Detect deterioration before hospitalization | Prevent high-cost inpatient claims |

### 3. Actuarial & Pricing

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Structured actuarial data feeds | Direct integration with pricing models | Move from population averages to individual trajectories |
| Continuous monitoring data | Forward-looking vs. backward-looking data | Price renewals accurately; reduce adverse selection |
| Health trajectory analysis | See direction of health, not just current state | Predict future claims more accurately |
| Longitudinal data ownership | Years of individual health data | Unprecedented pricing precision |
